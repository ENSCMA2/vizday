Frequently Asked Questions

What is this application for?

Viz is a website that helps parents/guardians to plan out their children's schedules weekly schedules. This application is intended to allow younger kids an opportunity to become more involved with planning out their schedules, and allows them to anticipate the days to come.

Who is the target audience?

The target audience of this application is parents/guardians who have children with autism. In many cases, a visual schedule allows parents to help their children increase their independence and managing anxiety.

Why did we decide to create this application?

We realized that we can help others who have anxiety to feel more freedom and accomplish more of what they want to achueve. One of our groupmates is very close to someone with autism, who writes out schedules for himself on a daily basis. We decided to create a online tool in order to help reach more people with this simple, quick organizational solution.

Who can use this application?

Although this application was mainly created with the needs of children with autism in mind, anyone child can benefit from using this schedule maker to map out their week.
